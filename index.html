How this works: Weather is fetched live from Open-Meteo for each campground's coordinates. Nearest-national-park distance is calculated from each site's coordinates against 40+ NPS park locations. Star ratings and review counts are pulled from Google's business data, and the 🏆 badge marks the highest Google-rated campground in each state. Each card links to its real official park/agency page — always confirm current cost and open dates directly on Recreation.gov or Hipcamp before booking, since public campgrounds fill up fast in peak season.
